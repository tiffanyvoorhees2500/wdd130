Rafting Site Plan

Tiffany Voorhees

Overview

Purpose

Create a web site for scheduling individuals/groups for guided rafting tours. The site will also explain to people new to rafting what it is and how it can be for individuals/groups of all skill levels.

Audience

  1. Who are the target Customers?
  2. What are they looking for?
    • Individuals/groups looking to explore the thrills of what we call whitewater rapids. These are people highly familiar with thrill seeking activities, and only require the guide to show them where to go rather than focusing on keeping them safe, while safetly is still a priority. These people would be taken down our rougher trails.
    • Individuals/groups looking for a relaxing journey down the river. These may be people more interested in the beauty of the land/water around them, rather than the excitement of white water rapids.
    • Large groups looking for an activity that people of all ages can participate in (EX: Family Reunions). This will be a group where safety will be high priority, as they will most likely have kids of all ages.
    • Independent People of all skill levels. EX: This could be an individual looking for something fun to outdoors. They may or may not have tried this type of activity before.
  3. Where will people be looking at the site?
    • Desktop
    • Laptop
    • Tablet
    • Mobile Devices
